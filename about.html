About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Asanas Yoga Studio, a sanctuary for self-discovery and transformation. Our journey began with a simple belief: that yoga is for everyone. Founded in 2010 by Shubhasri Saha, a passionate yogi with years of experience, the studio was born out of a desire to create a space where people from all walks of life could experience the profound benefits of yoga.

From our humble beginnings in a small shared space, we've grown into a thriving community, offering a diverse range of classes, workshops, and teacher training programs. Our commitment to authentic teaching, personalized attention, and a supportive environment has remained unwavering. We believe in fostering a sense of belonging, encouraging students to connect with their bodies, minds, and spirits.

Our Philosophy

At Asanas, we believe that yoga is more than just physical postures; it's a holistic practice that encompasses mindfulness, breathwork, and self-awareness. We embrace a philosophy that honors the ancient traditions of yoga while adapting them to the needs of the modern individual.

We strive to create a non-judgmental and inclusive space where students can explore their own unique path, free from competition and comparison. Our classes are designed to cultivate strength, flexibility, and balance, but more importantly, to promote inner peace, resilience, and a deeper connection to oneself. We are dedicated to empowering our students to live with intention, compassion, and joy, both on and off the mat.
